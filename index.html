How to delete your account

In-App Instant

1Open the TryWare AI app on your device.
2Tap the Settings tab in the bottom navigation bar.
3Scroll down to the Account section.
4Tap Delete Account (shown in red).
5Read the warning and tap Delete Permanently to confirm.

Email Request ≤ 7 days

1Send an email to our support address below.
2Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
3Include the email address linked to your TryWare AI account.
4We will confirm deletion within 7 business days.

Before you delete — please note

  • All remaining purchased credits will be permanently forfeited (non-refundable).
  • Your complete try-on history and generated images on our servers will be erased.
  • This action cannot be undone. There is no grace period or recovery option.
  • Re-registering with the same email will not restore credits or history.

About the email fingerprint: We retain a one-way cryptographic hash (HMAC-SHA256) of your email address after deletion. This hash cannot be reversed to reveal your email and contains zero personal information. It exists solely to prevent free-trial abuse — re-registering will not grant new free credits. This practice is GDPR-compliant and fully aligned with Google Play and Apple App Store policies.
