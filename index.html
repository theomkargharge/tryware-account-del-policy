How to Delete Your Account

📱 In-App Instant

  1. Open the TryWare AI app on your device.
  2. Tap the Settings tab in the bottom navigation bar.
  3. Scroll down to the ACCOUNT section.
  4. Tap Delete Account (shown in red).
  5. Read the warning and tap Delete Permanently to confirm.

✉️ Email Request ≤ 7 days

  1. Send an email to our support address below.
  2. Use subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the email address linked to your TryWare AI account.
  4. We will confirm deletion within 7 business days.

Before you delete — please note:

  • All remaining purchased credits will be permanently forfeited (non-refundable).

About the Email Fingerprint: We retain a one-way cryptographic hash (HMAC-SHA256) of your email address after deletion.
